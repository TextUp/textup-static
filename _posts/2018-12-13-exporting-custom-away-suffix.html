New TextUp features

  • Exporting records: Export client communications records to pdf files. Use it for reporting to supervisors, uploading to EHRs, and more.
  • Customizing mandatory away message: Administrators are now able to customize (or remove) the mandatory portion of the away message.

Note: Apple iOS devies (iPhone and iPad) do not support file exporting. However, you can access this feature in desktop view or on other types of smart phones.
